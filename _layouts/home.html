The purpose of this site is to raise awareness about the spread of invasive species and the harm they cause to the environment. Invasive species are an ever growing threat to our world in the way they reduce biodiversity and disrupt ecosystems and food chains. This site is organized into five main areas of content: podcasts, feature stories, infographic, glossary, and team bios.These can be navigated using the labeled navigation bar near the top of the page. Each section offers either insight into invasive species or information about the team that put this site together.
